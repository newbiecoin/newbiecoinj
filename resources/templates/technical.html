Description

Newbiecoin (NEWB) is a protocol, coin, and client used to run decentralized applications such as creating and backing decentralized crowdfunding projects , buying/selling in a decentralized exchange, etc. The protocol is built on top of the Bitcoin blockchain. Coins were created by burning Bitcoins during a creative "Twin Proof of Burn" period.

Newbiecoin is modified from ChanceCoin's open source. (Thanks Magician and Venetian . Here is more about ChanceCoin : https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=528023.0).

Newbiecoin Decentralized Crowdfunding Platform(NDCP)

Like other crowdfunding platforms such as Kickstarter, NDCP will serve as a means for users to create projects and pledge funds to others. However, websites like Kickstarter can charge as much as 10% of a project¡¯s total funds, making it harder for projects to successfully raise enough money. In NDCP , there is ZERO charge fee!

In many cases, centralized platforms impose restrictions on project creators that add to the burden of their task. Decentralization, on the other hand, empowers them to raise funds in a more proactive manner.

From a security perspective, NDCP protects users by not hosting private keys on any centralized server. This makes the platform a poor target for hackers or other exploiters.

Decentralized crowdfunding allows for more comprehensive vetting processes. In turn, this helps projects prove themselves to possible donors and increase their chances of success.

NDCP will support newbiecoin transactions and could lead to a reshaping of the peer-to-peer finance visio.

Maybe it will help us find more advanced features and services within the Newbiecoin/Bitcoin hybrid-network.
